Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to enhance your experience

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how D4Rent ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and provide you with a better browsing experience. Cookies can be temporary (session cookies) or permanent (persistent cookies).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide enhanced features.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies track your visit across our website and help us deliver relevant advertisements.

Cookie Management

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may affect your experience of our website. To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that use cookies on our website, including:

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis
  • Social media plugins for content sharing
  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Marketing and advertising partners

Your Choices

When you first visit our website, you will be prompted to accept or decline cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by:

  • Adjusting your browser settings
  • Using private/incognito browsing mode
  • Clearing your browser cookies
  • Opting out of specific third-party cookies

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
