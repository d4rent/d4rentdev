    Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

    Introduction

    At D4Rent, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

    Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information:

    • Personal details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
    • Property preferences and search criteria.
    • Technical data such as your IP address and browser type.

    How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:

    • Provide our services, including property listings and inquiries.
    • Communicate with you regarding your inquiries or transactions.
    • Improve our website and services.

    Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

    • Service providers who assist us in delivering our services.
    • Legal authorities if required by law.

    Your Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access the personal data we hold about you.
    • Request corrections to your personal data.
    • Request the deletion of your personal data.
